\section*{Problem}
We say that an element $e$ of a ring is $idempotent$ if $e^2=e$.\\
Prove that the set of all $idempotent$ elemnets of $M_2(\mathbb{R})$ is
\[
\left\{
\begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 0 \\
    0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
,
\begin{bmatrix}
    0 & 0 \\
    0 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
,
\begin{bmatrix}
    a & b \\
    c & 1-a
\end{bmatrix}
\mid
a,b,c \in \mathbb{R}
\text{ such that }
bc = a(1-a)
\right\}
\]
