\section*{Problem}
Let $R$ be a ring with unity $1_R$ such that $1_R\neq 0_R$ and let $a \in R$.\\
Prove that
\begin{enumerate}
    \item If $a$ is an unit, then $a$ is not a zero divisor.
    \item If $a$ is not a zero divisor and $ab=1_R$ for some $b\in R$, then $ba=1_R$
\end{enumerate}
